Health cAIre

AI-powered health application making personal health data understandable and actionable.

Complete design process from ideation through user research, wireframing, and final UI system. Focus on delivering motivating insights through clean, accessible interfaces.

UI/UX Design Mobile App Health Tech AI

BeeSmart

Digital co-pilot for beekeeping combining IoT technology with intuitive design.

Features mite tracking, treatment documentation, and AI-based health evaluation. Designed for both beginners and experienced beekeepers with trend-based insights.

IoT Mobile App Data Viz AgTech

Smart Glasses for the Blind

IoT solution enhancing social participation for visually impaired individuals through AI-powered environmental perception.

Developed in the Redesign course, featuring a Raspberry Pi 4 with camera and touch sensor. The system uses OpenAI's image analysis to generate descriptive audio through bone-conduction headsets, allowing users to perceive their environment while maintaining awareness of surrounding sounds.
